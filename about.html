'Tree Huggers' was founded 4 days ago when the co-founders, Sam and Frances, wanted to make a change. With the ever increasing use of technology, population growth and global warming - they decided that people were spending too much time indoors and that there were not enough green spaces in their local communities. They started by planting 100,000 trees - but soon realised that this was just scratching the surface of what was needed. They decided that they would get the public involved in their endeavors and incorporate friendly competition into the 'Green Leaves Scheme'.
